###Line Commands

There are five line commands.

- `M x y` (`m x y`) move to point
- `L x y` (`l x y`) line to point
- `H x` (`h dx`) horizontal line to point
- `V y` (`v dy`) vertical line to point
- `Z` (`z` is the same) close path
